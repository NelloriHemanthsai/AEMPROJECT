Lesson Overview

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) 6.5 is a comprehensive content management solution designed by Adobe to help organizations create, manage, and optimize digital customer experiences across various channels, including web, mobile, email, and forms. Here are some key features and aspects of AEM 6.5:

  • Content Management: AEM provides robust tools for managing digital content, including rich media, documents, and web pages.
  • Digital Asset Management (DAM): AEM includes DAM capabilities that facilitate the management and delivery of digital assets like images, videos, and documents.
  • Personalization and Targeting: AEM enables marketers to deliver personalized content to users based on their behavior, preferences, and demographics.
  • Multi-Site Management: Organizations can manage multiple websites and digital properties from a single instance of AEM.
